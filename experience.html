I was president silat for 3 years and 1 year as secretary
There were 3 categories which are single known as "Solo", partner known as "Tempur" and group known as "Masal". I took part in "Tempur" or partner category
We went for competition for state area. We will practice at the school or at Sekolah Agama Izzuddin Shah for the competition
My friend and I won 2 times at 2011 and 2013 for the second places
Not only for competition, we also do performance at school for some event which it was fun and created memories at school






As for the last sem, I involved with 'tarik tali' competition as for sports between faculty
